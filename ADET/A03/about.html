"With Great Power, There Must Also Come Great Responsibility"

- Aunt May

This timeless quote isn't just for Spider-Man — it reflects a universal truth about life. Every skill we learn, every talent we develop, and every role we take on gives us a form of power. Whether it's coding a system that people will rely on, sharing knowledge, or even just being a good friend — we all hold some kind of influence.

In human life, especially in tech and leadership, our "powers" aren't webs or super strength. They're our decisions, our actions, and how we choose to use our abilities. Responsibility means not just doing what's expected, but also doing what's right — even when no one's watching.

For me, this quote is a reminder to stay grounded as I grow — in tech, in life, and in purpose. Because the more I learn, the more I owe it to myself and to others to use that knowledge wisely.

About this Website

This website was personally designed by me for academic purposes. As a tribute to the legacy of Spider-Man and my love for the character, I created this project using the skills I have learned from Web Development. While it may not feature every possible layout or functionality, I focused on delivering a clean and simple design that showcases Spider-Man's iconic suits in a Bento-style format. This project serves as both a learning milestone and a creative expression of my passion for web development and the Spider-Man universe.
